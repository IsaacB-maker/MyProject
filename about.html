Our Story

More than just a salon - we're a community dedicated to the art and culture of dreadlocks.

How We Started

Founded in 2025, David's Mane began as a small studio with a big vision: to creat a safe, welcoming space wher everyone could embrace their natural hair journey. What started as a passion project has grown into a beloved salon known for its expertise and inclusive atmosphere

Our founder, David, started His own dreadlock journey over 15 years ago and discoverd a lack of specializedcare in the industry. He dedicated himself to mastering the craft and sharing his knowledge with others

Today, we serve hundreds of clients from all walks of life, helping them, beutiful dreadlocks that reflect their unique style and personality

Our Mission

At David's Mane, we believe that dredlocks are more than just a hairstyle - they're a statement of identity, culture, and personal expression. Our mission is to provide exceptional care and craftmanship while honoring the rich cultural heritageof lockedhair.

We're commited to creating a welcoming environment where everyone feels respected, valued, and beautiful in their own skin.
